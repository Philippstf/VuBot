Interoperable Approach to AV Distribution & Video Wall Management
VuTrex is the architecture and methodology that is at the core of every software component in the VuWall Ecosystem, bringing efficiency to your entire workflow. VuTrex is responsible for the ecosystem’s interoperability, expandability and simple configuration, without the need for programming.
Interoperable
Seamless integration to reduce deployment time and risk.
Hybrid
Handle a variety of source types and display formats.
Scalable
Modular architecture to future-proof your deployment.
No Programming
Configuration and operation with easy drag & drop operations.
