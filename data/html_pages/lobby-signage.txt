Lobby Signage Video Walls
Manage and Distribute Content Across Multi-Site Organizations
Book a demo
Engage and inform audiences on video walls or digital signs of any size and any aspect ratio, creating captivating experiences for employees throughout the organization and guests in corporate lobbies. Distribute content across multiple displays throughout facilities seamlessly with TRx, an easy-to-use centralized platform.
Centralized Management
Configure and operate a mix of video walls, multi-viewers and digital signs, deployed within one building or across your campus, with TRx - the centralized management platform. TRx can manage a mix of high and low resolution or bandwidth devices, along with multiple video wall controllers from a single interface.
Multi-Format Support
Future-proof your video wall deployment with TRx - a standards-based control system that is built on an open architecture. Easily integrate with your existing infrastructure and rest assured of the system’s interoperability with future technologies. TRx already supports H.264, SDVoE and SRT standards.
Emergency Messaging
Inform employees and visitors about important events. Launch presets based on trigger sensors, like movement, intrusion, or fire alarms. Quickly send out emergency messaging to all selected displays from a central location.
Powerful Graphics
Captivate your audience with the most brilliant visual experience on our high performance graphics cards. Our software makes it easy to manage complex layouts with different display sizes and aspect ratios, giving you the utmost flexibility with easy drag and drop operations.
Local Control
Give content managers easy-to-use local control with ControlVu, our fully customizable control panel. Create custom presets without any programming skills and with its easy-to-use interface, no training is required.
Exceptional Support
Our team of qualified video wall experts, located all over the world, are always available to help. In addition to our own technical support team, we have over 50 VuWall Certified Partners in 20 countries, giving you global access to technical support.
Use Cases
Easily manage and distribute AV content for lobby signage ad-hoc or with automated scheduling throughout multi-site organizations.

Corporate Communications
