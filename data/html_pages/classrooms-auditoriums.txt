Video Wall Solutions for Classrooms & Auditoriums
Enabling educators and presenters to create captivating and engaging experiences for their audiences.
VuWall simplifies AV deployments throughout campuses, giving students and teachers an easy way to share any content on any display on the campus network. Integrate easily with existing IT infrastructure and learning tools. Share content securely and easily with the utmost flexibility.
Flexibility
Display content on virtually any type of screen, video wall or projector and include content from as many sources as you need, thanks to our unique VuTrex technology. Our system can process any AV or IP feeds from cameras, web apps, computers, media players, etc. Our solution give educators the opportunity to be creative with their presentation material.
Multi-Format Support
Future-proof your video wall deployment with TRx - a standards-based control system that is built on VuTrex technology - an open, hybrid and interoperable architecture. Easily integrate with your existing infrastructure and rest assured of the system’s interoperability with future technologies.
IT Friendly
Our solution is web-based and users do not require any software installation. It is built on an open architecture offering interoperability and hybrid technology. It is secure with User Rights Management, Active Directory, and AES-128 encryption.
Powerful Graphics
Captivate your students with the most brilliant visual experience on our high performance graphics cards. Our software makes it easy to manage complex layouts with different display sizes and aspect ratios, giving you the utmost flexibility with easy drag and drop operations.
User-Friendly Control
Give teachers easy-to-use control with ControlVu, our fully customizable control panel. Create custom presets without any programming skills and allow teachers to control presentation content on the fly with a simple touch interface.
Exceptional Support
Our team of qualified video wall experts, located all over the world, are always available to help. In addition to our own technical support team, we have over 50 VuWall Certified Partners in 20 countries, giving you global access to technical support.
