VuWall Certifications
Ensuring Quality Throughout our Products and Services
At VuWall, product quality and reliability is our priority. Our team is dedicated to providing the highest level of quality and service that exceeds our clients’ expectations. Our product ecosystem, professional services and internal processes consistently meets and exceeds the stringent requirements established by internationally recognized standards.
SOC 2
Systems and processes designed to meet the requirements of SOC2 compliance, assuring prioritized security, confidentiality, and privacy, while undergoing rigorous auditing.
Read Security Statement
ISO CERTIFICATION
Quality Management System supporting the production, operation installation and servicing of VuWall’s entire product line as well as continually improving the process.
TRADE AGREEMENT ACT
TAA-compliant solutions that are built using premium components and manufactured under stringent quality guidelines and fully tested for compatibility.
NDAA
Products and services adhere to the regulations, guaranteeing compliance by utilizing premium components, quality control measures, and comprehensive compatibility testing.
Certification Program
VuWall offers a comprehensive CTS-accredited training for system designers, technical sales, consultants, and field and service technicians. The training is designed for qualified VuWall resellers who would like to design and service VuWall solutions. The training is offered several times each year at VuWall’s Montreal and Stuttgart locations or online. Resellers who complete the training can earn up to 12 renewal units (RUs) towards their AVIXA Certified Technology Specialist (CTS) certification.
