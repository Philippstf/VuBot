Training
VuWall offers a comprehensive CTS-accredited training for system designers, technical sales, consultants, and field and service technicians. The training is designed for qualified VuWall resellers who would like to design and service VuWall solutions. This course is offered several times each year at VuWall’s Canadian and German locations or online. Resellers who complete the training can earn up to 12 renewal units (RUs) towards their AVIXA Certified Technology Specialist (CTS) certification.
Our certification program includes 3 levels:
Level I: VuWall Designer (1 day – 4 RUs)
Level II: VuWall Field Technician
(
1 day – 4 RUs)
Level III: VuWall Service Specialist (1 day – 4 RUs)
