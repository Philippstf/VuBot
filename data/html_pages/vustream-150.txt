Delivering high-quality video streams in control rooms
The VuStream 150 encoder supports HDMI signals at resolutions up to 3840×2160@60Hz, 4:4:4 pass-through, 4:2:0 and 4:2:2 encoding & streaming, and PoE. It is compatible with PAK KVM and Video Wall Nodes, VuScape Controllers, and common software decoders like VLC.
