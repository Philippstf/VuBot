Technical Support
VuWall believes in supporting and empowering our users through every step of their journey with our products. To that end, we offer a number of free and premium services to help you get the most from our powerful suite of video wall solutions.
Training Services
End User Training
VuWall is committed to ensuring that end users use our visualization solutions to their fullest potential. Our products are designed to be extremely intuitive and user-friendly. Still, with so many powerful features, our end user training program includes an in-depth look at both administrative and operational functionality. Our qualified instructors customized every training session to the client’s needs, depending on their operations and workflows.
Partner Training & Certification Program
VuWall offers a comprehensive CTS-accredited training for system designers, technical sales, consultants, and field and service technicians. The training is available to qualified VuWall resellers and consultants who would like to design and service VuWall solutions. Earn up to 12 renewal units (RUs) towards their AVIXA Certified Technology Specialist (CTS) certification.
