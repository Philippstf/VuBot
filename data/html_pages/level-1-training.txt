Certification Training - Level 1
Sign up for live online or in-person training and become a Certified VuWall Specialist.
Duration: 1 day (8 hours)
CTS RU Credits: 4
SIGN UP
Course Description
Upon completion of this course, graduates will be able to present VuWall’s entire line of solutions to potential end users and assist in designing a video wall, IP KVM, and AV distribution project. The course includes thorough examples of customer installations and detailed training on both software and hardware features and functionalities. This 1-day course is broken down into several modules including management software, PAK distributed video wall & KVM nodes, custom video wall controllers, encoders & decoders, and extended features such as color detection, touch panel control, VMS integrations, and more. The course also includes understanding site requirements.
This course will include a series of short quizzes that will require an 80% passing grade. Following a passing grade, trainees will receive a Certificate from VuWall that shall be submitted by the trainee to AVIXA in order to receive their CTS RU credits.

Course Objectives
Understand and present VuWall’s complete line of solutions, both hardware and software, along with their differentiators, benefits, and use cases.
Design video wall, IP KVM, and AV distribution projects with VuWall solutions.
Specify the right VuStream encoders and decoders, PAK and VuScape video wall processors, TRx Centralized Management System, along with other peripherals that may be required.
