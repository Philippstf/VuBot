Looking for a new challenge in a fast-growing, dynamic high-tech company? Join our team!
VuWall is headquartered in Montreal with a European subsidiary near Stuttgart, Germany and in Atlanta, GA, USA. With customers in more than 45 countries, we have deployed over 5,000 projects in many Fortune 500 organizations, government agencies, utility, transport, and security companies.
We have a dynamic team of creative software specialists who are shaping the future of multi-surface computing. Our culture is diverse, collaborative, and always encouraging outside-the-box thinking. Everyone at VuWall appreciates their peers and the amazing work that goes into ensuring that our customers have the best visualization experience with our products and the best customer support.
