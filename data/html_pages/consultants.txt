Training
VuWall offers a comprehensive CTS-accredited training for consultants who would like to better understand how to design your projects with VuWall solutions. The training is offered several times each year at VuWall’s Canadian and German locations. Consultants who complete the training will earn 4 renewal units (RUs) toward AVIXA’s Certified Technology Specialist (CTS) credential.
