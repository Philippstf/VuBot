Enhancing your video wall deployments. Increasing productivity.
VuWall offers an array of interoperable products to support a complete integration for your video wall projects while reducing integration risk. All VuWall products are powered bu
VuTrex technology
for a seamlessly integrated visualization solution.

Application Server
Securely share and control websites, dashboards, and applications on video walls across AV/IT networks.
Learn More
Color Detection Server
Monitor unattended computers, video walls or virtual machines by automatically detecting color changes on multiple points (up to 50 points per computer)  and trigger events in TRx or third-party applications.
Color Detection Server Datasheet
V-IO Box
Capture a dry contact change from a sensor and convert it into a command to TRx thanks to our V-IO Box. Alternatively, close or open a dry contact from a TRx event or script.
V-IO Box Datasheet
KM Module
Keyboard and mouse emulator to add control to any source window on your video wall and turn your video wall into a large KVM multiviewer.
KM Module Datasheet
AdScape 4K Expander
Enhance the power of VuScape video wall controllers with AdScape to easily deploy stunning architectural video walls and non-rectangular display surfaces.
AdScape Datasheet
